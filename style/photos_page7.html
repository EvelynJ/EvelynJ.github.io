Tuesday 6th January

Death by fruit salad :-p

I went to the market today to get some fresh fruit and veg, and can't believe how cheap it all is compared to the supermarket, and how fresh. I got the bounty above for less than £4! The cherries were about 25p, the blueberries were 90p, the onions 50p, the garlic was 10p, and I splashed out £1.80 on the figs, which are beautiful (and which the stalls didn't sell in smaller quantities). I have spent a yummy afternoon just munching on the figs while I've unpacked some stuff. Heaven. it's definitely worth the half hour on the crowded metro for such good food.
